Section 01

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Endure ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the Endure mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Endure is a behavioral training app designed to help users improve patience, impulse control, focus, and calmness through interactive mini-games. We have designed the App with a privacy-first approach, storing your data locally on your device whenever possible.

Section 02

Information We Collect

2.1 — Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device using Android DataStore and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Game performance data — scores, coins, best times, accuracy percentages, depth records, and completion rates for each game mode (Wait or Tap, Mind Thread, Schulte Table, Breath Hold, Catch Game).
  • Progression data — XP, level, patience score, reflex score, and focus score.
  • Streak information — current streak, best streak, streak freezes, and daily challenge progress.
  • User preferences — reminder settings, sound preferences, and notification preferences.
  • Nickname — an auto-generated or user-set display name for leaderboard purposes.
  • Pro subscription status — active subscription or pack status, type, and expiry date.
  • Daily challenge data — current challenges, progress, completion, and bonus claim status.
  • Behavioral data — tap timing patterns used to detect rage taps for in-game scoring purposes only.

2.3 — What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your real name, email, phone number, or address. We do not require account registration. We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, photos, or files. We do not collect precise GPS location. We do not track browsing activity outside the App. We do not sell any user data to third parties.

Section 03

How We Use Information

Locally stored data is used exclusively for:

  • Providing core App functionality — tracking your game progress, scores, and achievements.
  • Progression system — calculating XP, levels, streaks, and daily challenge completion.
  • Personalization — adjusting game difficulty based on your level and displaying your nickname.
  • Notifications — sending daily reminders at your preferred time (if enabled).
  • Subscription management — determining ad display, premium level access, and Pro features.
  • Behavioral scoring — using tap timing data to calculate patience and impulse control scores.

Section 06

Data Retention & Deletion

Since all user data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data persists as long as the App is installed.
  • Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all locally stored data.
  • Clearing App data/cache in device settings also deletes all stored data.
  • We have no ability to recover deleted data, as it is not stored on our servers.

For third-party service data retention, please refer to Google's privacy policies.
